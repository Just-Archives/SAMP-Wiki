Available Player Skins

There are 311 skins available in SA-MP, including skins for public services like the police, firemen, ambulance personnel but also much different gangs, people from a specific profession and normal pedestrians. A full, graphical reference including large pictures is available on GTA Tools, provided by JernejL. This, together with the following pages, will provide a categorized overview of all pedestrians.

Note

In new versions of SA-MP since 0.3.7 RC3 you can use more than 299 skins, maximum 311 skins.
